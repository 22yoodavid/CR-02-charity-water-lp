Meet Amina

Every morning, Amina set out before dawn to fetch water—dirty, unsafe water. But with your help, everything has changed. A new well means clean water flows freely in her village. Amina has time to learn, play, and dream bigger than ever. Now, instead of spending hours on dangerous journeys, she walks just a few steps to fill her container. Her brothers and sisters no longer miss school because of waterborne illnesses. Clean water hasn’t just improved daily life—it’s opened the door to a healthier, brighter future for her entire community. Your support makes stories like Amina’s possible. Together, we can bring safe, reliable water to families still waiting.
